Sparq Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

App ID: com.sparq.adhd

Sparq ("we", "our", "us") is a gamified ADHD companion app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users, especially those in neurodivergent communities. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

Account data (when you sign up):

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (if you sign in via Google or Apple)

App usage data (stored on your device and/or our servers):

  • XP, level, streak, and quest completion stats
  • Journal entries and mood check-ins
  • Community posts and interactions (likes, cheers)
  • Safety actions (reports, blocks)

We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone number
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health data beyond what you voluntarily enter (mood, journal)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the app experience (XP tracking, pet evolution, streaks)
  • To display your posts in the community feed
  • To process safety reports and enforce community guidelines
  • To send optional push notifications (reminders, streak alerts)
  • To improve the app through aggregated, anonymized usage analytics

3. Data Storage and Security

Account authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC). Your auth credentials are encrypted and managed by Firebase under their privacy policy.

App data is stored locally on your device (via localStorage) and, when connected to an account, synced to Firebase servers located in the United States. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication — login and account management
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — optional social login providers
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore — data storage (when implemented)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Sparq is designed for users aged 13 and older. Users under 13 must have a parent or guardian set up and supervise their account.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided personal data without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

Parents can contact us at any time to review, delete, or restrict the processing of their child's data.

6. Community Safety

Sparq includes safety features designed to protect users:

  • Content filter that detects and flags grooming language and personal information sharing
  • Report system — all reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • Block functionality to remove unwanted users from your feed
  • Safe DMs (on by default) — only approved contacts can message you
  • Private Mode — hide your profile from community search

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via in-app notification. Continued use of Sparq after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
